As a general rule, verbs usually end in "de1" if they're simple, if they're more complex and have multiple meanings attached to it they may have "de1" somewhere in the verb, such as the middle.

A verb in this context is either an active action or state of being.


| Romanization | Definition                                                                  |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| tyi3de1      | To exist as, to be. Eq. "am"<br>ex. bahkae kae tyide (Tall I am, I am tall) |
